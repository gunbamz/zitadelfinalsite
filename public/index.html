A certified healthcare and multi-diagnostic centre with special interest in accurate diagnosis, quality clinical services. At Zitadelmedics, we put your well-being first! Our highly trained and specialized team are ready 24/7 to deliver quality services. ZitadelMedics is equipped with state-of-arts medical and diagnostic equipments.
We are your partner in progressive health care.

Neurosurgery

We deliver complex neurosurgical services (Brain & Spine Surgery) with good outcomes. This service is led by out in-house Consultant Neurosurgeon and supported by our partnership with Brain and Spine Surgery Consortium (BASS)

Family Medicine

Briefly about Our Clinic

We are committed to discouraging medical tourism. Nigerians do not have to travel abroad for complex surgeries. We currently have three centers across nigeria: Abuja, Port Harcourt and Uyo. We are also listed among Health Care Providers for most HMOs in Nigeria.

We strive to promote healthy life style by the occasional free medical councelling and screeing against common diseases.

Our Phamacy unit is available 24hrs. They are relatively cheap and cost effective.

We offer 24 hours services with fully equipped emergency unit. Our Ambulance is always on stand-by!

We strive to treat each child and each family member the way we wish to be treated.

Utilizing the latest advances in medicine combined with the state-of-the-art techniques.

We perform image-guided procedures for minimal invasive surgery tailored to meet your needs.
