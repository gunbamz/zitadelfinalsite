Neurosurgery clinic

  • General Info
  • Doctors List
  • Timetable

The Neurosurgery clinic is a multi-disciplinary clinic which includes consultant neurosurgeons, neuropsychologists, physiotherapists and cancer support staff.

We deliver complex neurosurgical services (Brain & Spine Surgery) with good outcomes. This service is led by out in-house Consultant Neurosurgeon and supported by our partnership with Brain and Spine Surgery Consortium (BASS).
All surgeons specialise in different areas including:

  • Cerebrovascular surgery, including aneurysms
  • Brain tumour and skull base surgery
  • neuro-endoscopic surgery
  • complex spine and cranial microsurgery
  • neuro-oncology
  • fistulae
  • cavernomas
  • carotid occlusive disease
  • trigeminal neuralgia
  • degenerative spinal disease

The eye clinic of Zitadel Medicals and Diagnostics Limited is a fully equipped eye and vision care centre.Our services are centered on diagnosis, quality clinical services and examination which is critical to determining our patient's optimum vision and eye care and subsequent management of the conditions detected.

We are committed to building a good reputation in the field of ophthalmic services and becoming a key player in the eye care industry, thereby reducing to the barest minimum, every preventable causes of blindness and also providing world class, compassionate, quality eye/vision care and accurate diagnosis that is very much affordable.
