Neurosurgery clinic

  • General Info

The Neurosurgery clinic is a multi-disciplinary clinic which includes consultant neurosurgeons, neuropsychologists, physiotherapists and cancer support staff.

We deliver complex neurosurgical services (Brain & Spine Surgery) with good outcomes. This service is led by out in-house Consultant Neurosurgeon and supported by our partnership with Brain and Spine Surgery Consortium (BASS).
All surgeons specialise in different areas including:

  • Cerebrovascular surgery, including aneurysms
  • Brain tumour and skull base surgery
  • neuro-endoscopic surgery
  • complex spine and cranial microsurgery
  • neuro-oncology
  • fistulae
  • cavernomas
  • carotid occlusive disease
  • trigeminal neuralgia
  • degenerative spinal disease
